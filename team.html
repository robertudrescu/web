Some important characters

The story is full of amazing characters. Here are a few of them.

Lati

lati.înțeleptul@poveste.clr

𝕷𝕬𝕿𝕴𝕴𝕹𝕿𝕰𝕷𝕰𝕻𝕿𝖀𝕷

Lati is the wisest and most handsome person, who also has a massive nose. He is very strong and excellent at telling truthful stories about himself.

Capra

capra.oaia@tragelacăcăreze.clr

𝕮𝕬𝕻𝕽𝕬

Capra is a sheep or a goat that is Lati's mount. He has wings to fly and is able to speak and throw his horns like boomerangs.

Trompompom

trompompom@pompom.pom

𝕿𝕽𝕺𝕸𝕻𝕺𝕸𝕻𝕺𝕸

Trompompom is always hungry and grows when he eats and shrinks when he is hungry until he reaches a point where he has shrunk as much as he is able to. He can bite very hard and his teeth are extremely hard and grow back if they break. His folk is originally from the fifth floor.

Nefinfir

nefinfir@odf.et4

𝕹𝕰𝕱𝕴𝕹𝕱𝕴𝕽

Nefinfir is an old and wise man who lives on the fourth floor. He has the ability to shrink objects small enough.

Cap Mare

cap.mare@atrocia.clr

𝕮𝕬𝕻𝕸𝕬𝕽𝕰

Cap Mare is an atroc that was captured by the evil turtle Zaran. He eats a lot of eggs and his head grows as he eats.

Domnu' Ciupercă Mare

ciupercă.mare@keiemic.clr

𝕯𝕺𝕸𝕹𝖀𝕮𝕴𝖀𝕻𝕰𝕽𝕮𝕬𝕸𝕬𝕽𝕰

Domnu' Ciupercă mare(a.k.a. Mr. Big Mushroom) is a great warrior who protects the small key to enter the castle. He has a mushroom-shaped helmet, a big hammer and a big cat.

Some villains

Broasca Neagra

link

Broasca Neagra(a.k.a. The Black Frog) is one of the two main villains on the third floor and the leader of all the wicked frogs. He is a huge dark-colored frog with armour and spikes on its back and mainly uses his mace as a weapon.

Zaran

link

Zaran is an evil turtle that lives in the subterranean land of the atrocs on the fifth floor. Even as a turtle he doesn't look much like one, for example, he has wings, cat-like eyes and a humanoid body. He wants to explore all the other lands and also destroy the town of the atrocs.
