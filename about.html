The Red Dragon in the Walnut Shell

On the third floor, Lati, Capra and Trompompom went together to the three strange and obscure islands, the one of ice, the one of fog, and the one of fire, looking for the entrance to the fourth floor. On the Island of Ice, he discovered a map that showed that there was something on the Island of Fire, which he believed was the entrance to the fourth floor. Also on the Island of Ice Trompompom's pants froze and he got very sick. Before reaching the Islands of Fire, they passed through the Island of Fog where they encountered eagles with four wings. Lati and his company journeyed on to the Island of Fire to look for the enterance to the fourth floor. The area was volcanic and all burned up and the air rippled with heat. In the middle of the island there was an enormous volcano. The company went into the volcano. In the middle there was lava like in a boiling pot. Not far away from it was an unusually massive walnut shell, but they did not know what it was about. The map showed that the enterance was beneath the lava, but no one knew how to get to it without getting cooked. After pondering for some time, they headed back to the main land, hoping to find a solution to their problem. They arrived back to the desert where the abnormal-looking people lived in their floating towers. The frogs had already attacked them with their silverfish-like worms. The Black Frog was also there. Eventually they defeated the frogs with the help of the eagles, but the eagles had promised to help them on one condition. The eagles had long ago lost a huge walnut shell with golden feathers in it, which are very precious and grow extremely rarely on them. They really wanted it back, but couldn't find it. Lati and his company had to find its location. Lati remembered the huge walnut shell inside the volcano and told about it to the eagles. The eagles knew it was it. Some of the eagles flew with Lati to the volcano. Trompompom remained on the Island of Fog for his cold had got worse. The eagles arrived to the volcano and flew into it from the top of it. When they saw it they rejoiced. One of the eagles went near it, but at the same from the walnut shell came a fire that burned the eagle to ashes in an instant. The nutshell opened and from it came a terrible red dragon.
