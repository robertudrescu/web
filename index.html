Lati and the eight-story castle

Lati went with Capra to the enchanted land by accident and there he entered an eight-story castle. He had to get to the top floor, because someone said so. He managed to get to the second floor. From the second floor he got to the third one and after that to the fourth one and then to the fifth one.
